## ğŸ® How to Use

1. Open any crafting interface (Crafting Table, 2x2 inventory grid, etc.)
2. **Click on a recipe** in the Recipe Book to select it
3. **Press `Space` or `Enter`** to craft instantly
4. **Hold `Shift`** while pressing the key to craft multiple batches
5. Keep pressing to keep crafting!

**Pro Tip:** The mod remembers your last recipe, so you can close the Recipe Book and still use the shortcut!
